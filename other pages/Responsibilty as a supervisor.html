My Responsibilities as a Supervisor
in
LEXZYS RECYCLING COMPANY

  1. Supervising Operations: Overseeing the day-to-day activities of the recycling process, including sorting,
    cleaning, shredding, and melting plastic materials.

  2. Ensuring Safety: Implementing and enforcing safety protocols to ensure that employees operate machinery
    and handle materials safely. This includes providing appropriate safety equipment and training.

  3. Quality Control: Monitoring the quality of recycled plastic materials to ensure they meet industry standards and
    customer requirements. This may involve conducting inspections and implementing quality control procedures.

  4. Managing Employees: Training and managing a team of workers involved in the recycling process. This includes assigning
    tasks, providing feedback, and addressing any performance issues.
  5. Compliance: Ensuring compliance with environmental regulations and permits related to waste management and recycling activities.
    This includes proper handling and disposal of hazardous materials.

  6. Continuous Improvement: Identifying opportunities for process improvement and implementing measures to increase efficiency, reduce waste,
    and optimize recycling rates.

  7. Communication: Liaising with other departments within the company, as well as external stakeholders such as suppliers and customers,
    to coordinate activities and address any issues that may arise.

  8. Documentation: Maintaining accurate records and documentation related to production, quality control, safety, and compliance for
    regulatory purposes and internal reporting.
