Responsibilities As A Social Media Manager
And Customer Support
At People's Court 9ja

  • Social media Management


    • Developing and executing social media strategies to increase brand visibility, engagement, and followership across various platforms
      such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.

    • Creating, curating, and scheduling content for social media posts, including text, images, videos, and other multimedia formats,
      aligning with the company's branding and messaging guidelines.

    • Monitoring social media channels for mentions, comments, messages, and relevant conversations related to the brand or industry.

    • Engaging with the audience by responding to comments, messages, and inquiries in a timely and professional manner, fostering positive
      interactions and building relationships with followers.

    • Analyzing social media metrics and performance data to track the effectiveness of campaigns, identify trends, and make data-driven
      decisions to optimize content and strategies.


  • Customer Support


    • Providing timely and helpful responses to customer inquiries, concerns, and feedback received through social media platforms, email, chat,
      or other communication channels.

    • Addressing customer issues, resolving complaints, and providing solutions or assistance related to products, services, orders, billing,
      technical issues, etc.

    • Escalating complex or unresolved issues to appropriate departments or team members for further investigation and resolution.

    • Monitoring customer sentiment and feedback on social media platforms, identifying trends, patterns, and recurring issues to address
      systemic problems and improve customer satisfaction.

    • Collaborating with other departments such as marketing, sales, and product development to communicate customer feedback, advocate for
      customer needs, and contribute to the development of customer-centric solutions and strategies.

    • Documenting and maintaining records of customer interactions, inquiries, resolutions, and feedback in the customer relationship management
      (CRM) system or other relevant databases.
